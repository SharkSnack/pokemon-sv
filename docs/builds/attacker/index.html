Attacker

Edit on GitHub

These are the attacker pokemon builds I've made for Tera Raids. - ※ Last updated December 29th, 2022.

Stat abbreviations: Atk - Attack, Def - Defense, SpA - Sp. Atk, SpD - Sp. Def, Spe - Speed

Randoms

Builds for Online (Randoms)

Physical

Name Iron Hands Notes
Item Booster Energy / Life Orb
Black Belt / Sitrus Berry		 Electric Terrain activates Quark Drive
and grants sleep immunity to party.

If there are other belly drum users,
use Hang Tough! to reduce damage,
then Belly Drum after ally stats reset.

However, if there are Screech users,
it's fine to aim for the one-shot.

Swap Thunder Punch with Close
Combat if you want more power, but
then make a 2nd one for Electric.
Tera Fighting
Ability Quark Drive
Nature Adamant (+Atk, -SpA)
EVs 76 HP, 252 Atk, 4 Def, 176 SpD
Moves - Drain Punch
- Thunder Punch
- Belly Drum
- Electric Terrain

Name Azumarill Notes
Item Sitrus Berry / Shell Bell Tera Fairy is best for general use, but
Water is great for water-weak bosses
when combined with Rain Dance.

Rain Dance raises the accuracy of
Hurricane and Thunder to 100%.
Tera Fairy or Water
Ability Huge Power
Nature Adamant (+Atk, -SpA)
EVs 252 HP, 252 Atk, 4 Def
Moves - Play Rough
- Liquidation
- Belly Drum
- Rain Dance

Name Ceruledge Notes
Item Charcoal / Metronome Shadow Claw can be swapped with
Sunny Day or Reflect if desired.

Don't use Clear Smog if the boss has
been debuffed - Press Y to check if
you're unsure.

Great against Tauros (Blaze Breed)
and Flareon in particular.

Metronome is best for solo.
Tera Fire
Ability Flash Fire
Nature Adamant (+Atk, -SpA)
EVs 252 HP, 252 Atk, 4 Spe
Moves - Bitter Blade
- Shadow Claw
- Swords Dance
- Clear Smog

Name Annihilape Notes
Item Spell Tag / Clear Amulet
Shell Bell / Covert Cloak		 Rage Fist gains 50 BP each time you
get hit up to 350 BP, which doesn't
reset even if you faint.

Bulk Up is better for solo since NPCs
never use Heal Up!

Don't use Clear Amulet if you go with
Defiant for your ability.
Tera Ghost
Ability Vital Spirit / Defiant (HA)
Nature Adamant (+Atk, -SpA)
EVs 252 HP, 252 Atk, 4 Def
Moves - Rage Fist
- Drain Punch
- Screech / Bulk Up
- Taunt

Name Koraidon Notes
Item Black Belt / Life Orb Orichalcum Pulse sets sun on entry.

Breaking Swipe lowers Atk by 1 stage
even when the boss has a shield, but
can be swapped with Collision Course
for more power if you want.
Tera Fighting
Ability Orichalcum Pulse
Nature Adamant (+Atk, -SpA)
EVs 252 HP, 252 Atk, 4 SpD
Moves - Drain Punch
- Breaking Swipe
- Screech
- Swords Dance

Name Tinkaton Notes
Item Metal Coat Gigaton Hammer has 160 BP but can't
be used twice in a row, so the idea is to
sandwich other moves between uses.

That said, don't immediately attack the
boss without buffing yourself first.

Even better if there are Perrserkers.

234 speed lets you outspeed neutral
nature Maushold if you care about it.
Tera Steel
Ability Mold Breaker
Nature Adamant (+Atk, -SpA)
EVs 216 HP, 252 Atk, 36 Spe
Moves - Gigaton Hammer
- Play Rough
- Swords Dance
- Reflect / Light Screen

Name Cetitan Notes
Item Life Orb Sheer Force boosts power of moves
with secondary effects by x1.3 and
negates damage from Life Orb.

Other options include Heavy Slam,
Earthquake, and Superpower, but
none of these gain the power boost
from Sheer Force.
Tera Ice
Ability Sheer Force (HA)
Nature Adamant (+Atk, -SpA)
EVs 252 Atk, 128 Def, 128 SpD
Moves - Icicle Crash
- Play Rough
- Liquidation
- Belly Drum

Name Kingambit Notes
Item Shell Bell Simple attacker for ghost raids or such.

Taunt is a bit wasted unless you invest
EVs in speed, so Metal Sound is an
option to help special attackers.

Beware of Fighting type moves.
Tera Dark or Steel
Ability Defiant
Nature Adamant (+Atk, -SpA)
EVs 216 HP, 252 Atk, 36 Spe
Moves - Kowtow Cleave
- Iron Head
- Swords Dance
- Taunt / Metal Sound

Name Perrserker Notes
Item Wide Lens / Metal Coat Steely Spirit boosts the power of Steel
type attacks by x1.5 and stacks with
multiple Perrserkers.

Screech and Helping Hand are nice if
there's a Belly Drum user or such, but
Fake Tears is also an option if you
team with Magnezone or Gholdengo.

You could build it as a pure support
rather than an attacker if desired.
Tera Steel
Ability Steely Spirit (HA)
Nature Adamant (+Atk, -SpA)
EVs 252 HP, 252 Atk, 4 SpD
Moves - Iron Head
- Screech
- Swords Dance
- Helping Hand / Taunt

Name Iron Thorns Notes
Item Booster Energy Main attacks have low BP, but can hit
hard with some buffs / debuffs.

Include Earthquake or Iron Head if
you want more coverage.

Overall, not bad but doesn't stand out.
Tera Electric or Rock
Ability Quark Drive
Nature Adamant (+Atk, -SpA)
EVs 236 HP, 252 Atk, 20 SpD
Moves - Thunder Punch
- Rock Slide
- Screech
- Swords Dance

Name Garchomp Notes
Item Soft Sand / Shell Bell Simple attacker for Earth-weak bosses.

Not that good, honestly.
Tera Ground
Ability Sand Veil
Nature Adamant (+Atk, -SpA)
EVs 252 HP, 252 Atk, 4 SpD
Moves - Earthquake
- Dragon Claw
- Crunch
- Swords Dance

Name Charizard Notes
Item Sitrus Berry You'll faint if you use Flare Blitz.

It can one-shot Fire-weak bosses
in sunlight, but may need Screech.
Koraidon is a perfect partner.

Overall, not that good.
Tera Fire
Ability Blaze
Nature Adamant (+Atk, -SpA)
EVs 252 HP, 252 Atk, 4 Def
Moves - Acrobatics
- Fire Punch
- Flare Blitz
- Belly Drum

Special

Name Iron Moth Notes
Item Booster Energy Electric Terrain activates Quark Drive
and grants sleep immunity to party.

Acid Spray lowers SpD by 2 stages
even when the boss has a shield.

Fiery Dance has a 50% chance to raise
SpA by 1 stage each time you use it.

Tera Poison is great against Amoonguss.
Tera Poison or Fire
Ability Quark Drive
Nature Modest (+SpA, -Atk)
EVs 252 HP, 252 SpA, 4 Spe
Moves - Sludge Wave
- Fiery Dance
- Acid Spray
- Electric Terrain

Name Flutter Mane Notes
Item Booster Energy / Covert Cloak Mystical Fire lowers SpA by 1 stage
even when the boss has a shield.

Low HP and Def, so don't bring it to
a boss with strong physical attacks.
Tera Fairy
Ability Protosynthesis
Nature Modest (+SpA, -Atk)
EVs 252 HP, 252 SpA, 4 SpD
Moves - Moonblast
- Draining Kiss
- Fake Tears
- Mystical Fire

Name Sylveon Notes
Item Expert Belt / Covert Cloak You're unlikely to one-shot the boss
with Hyper Beam with randoms, so
don't bother trying for it.

Light Screen is better for party play,
but Calm Mind can be used once to
raise both SpA and SpD by 1 stage.

Don't waste lots of time doing buffs
and debuffs - Terastallizing is often
more important.
Tera Fairy
Ability Pixilate (HA)
Nature Modest (+SpA, -Atk)
EVs 252 HP, 252 SpA, 4 SpD
Moves - Hyper Voice
- Fake Tears
- Calm Mind / Light Screen
- Misty Terrain

Name Miraidon Notes
Item Magnet / Life Orb Miraidon outspeeds every boss that
has Taunt in its move set, so you can
Taunt to protect slower support allies.

Only use Metal Sound once unless
there are other special attackers.
It's important to Terastallize quickly.
Tera Electric
Ability Hadron Engine
Nature Modest (+SpA, -Atk)
EVs 252 HP, 252 SpA, 4 Def
Moves - Electro Drift
- Parabolic Charge
- Metal Sound
- Taunt / Reflect

Name Gholdengo Notes
Item Metal Coat Good as Gold protects against status
moves, but you lose it for 1 turn when
the boss does a player reset.

Don't get too greedy with buffs and
debuffs when playing with randoms.

Most bosses wipe buffs after 180 sec
and wipe debuffs at 50% HP.
Tera Steel
Ability Good as Gold
Nature Modest (+SpA, -Atk)
EVs 252 HP, 252 SpA, 4 Spe
Moves - Make It Rain
- Shadow Ball
- Nasty Plot
- Metal Sound

Name Magnezone Notes
Item Metal Coat Analytic boosts attack power by 30%
if you move last, so 0 Speed IV and
Quiet nature is best if you use it.

Metal Sound lowers SpD by 2 stages,
but doesn't work on shield.

Steel Beam is mainly only used when
aiming for a one-shot. Swap it with
an electric type attack if desired.
Tera Steel
Ability Analytic (HA)
Nature Quiet (+SpA, -Spe)
EVs 252 HP, 252 SpA, 4 Def
Moves - Flash Cannon
- Steel Beam
- Metal Sound
- Reflect / Light Screen

Name Bellibolt Notes
Item Metronome Electromorphosis grants charged
status when you get hit, boosting the
power of your next Electric type move
by 100% - But it doesn't raise SpD.

Support moves are included in case
the party is struggling, but prioritize
repeated Parabolic Charge usage
to build Metronome's power boost.

Also works pretty well for solo.
Tera Electric
Ability Electromorphosis
Nature Modest (+SpA, -Atk)
EVs 252 HP, 252 SpA, 4 Def
Moves - Parabolic Charge
- Acid Spray
- Reflect / Light Screen
- Electric Terrain

Name Espathra Notes
Item Shell Bell Lumina Crash lowers SpD by 2 stages
even when the boss has a shield.

Opportunist is better on paper, but
copying every Hone Claws, Bulk Up,
Dragon Dance, Swords Dance, etc.
wastes a lot of time, so I prefer Frisk.

Only use Skill Swap with Frisk.
Tera Psychic
Ability Frisk / Opportunist
Nature Modest (+SpA, -Atk)
EVs 252 HP, 252 SpA, 4 Def
Moves - Lumina Crash
- Reflect
- Light Screen
- Roost / Skill Swap

Name Charizard Notes
Item Shell Bell Solar Power boosts SpA by x1.5 in sun,
and sunlight boosts fire moves by x1.5,
so it's nice against Fire-weak bosses.

Charizard has low defenses, so use
Hang Tough! if no one else does.
Tera Fire
Ability Solar Power (HA)
Nature Modest (+SpA, -Atk)
EVs 252 HP, 252 SpA, 4 Def
Moves - Flamethrower
- Air Slash
- Blast Burn
- Sunny Day

Name Skeledirge Notes
Item Metronome / Shell Bell Torch Song raises SpA by 1 stage.

Unaware is a bit of a double-edged
sword since it ignores both buffs
and debuffs. Still, it's useful against
bosses that use Calm Mind, etc.

Will-O-Wisp is to reduce attack
power of physical bosses.
Tera Fire
Ability Unaware (HA)
Nature Modest (+SpA, -Atk)
EVs 252 HP, 252 SpA, 4 SpD
Moves - Torch Song
- Shadow Ball
- Earth Power
- Will-O-Wisp

Name Volcarona Notes
Item Shell Bell Flame Body can inflict burn even when
the boss has a shield.

Fiery Dance has a 50% chance to raise
SpA by 1 stage each time you use it.

Swap Light Screen with Quiver Dance
if you want a guaranteed power boost.

Screech is used to aim for a one-shot
if there's a Belly Drum user.
Tera Bug or Fire
Ability Flame Body
Nature Modest (+SpA, -Atk)
EVs 252 HP, 252 SpA, 4 Def
Moves - Bug Buzz
- Fiery Dance
- Light Screen
- Screech

For Fun

Name Zangoose Notes
Item Toxic Orb Hidden ability boosts Atk by x1.5 which
plays well with STAB Facade.

Zangoose can learn Belly Drum, but it's
far too squishy to utilize it.

※ Don't bring this to ★6~7 raids.
Tera Normal
Ability Toxic Boost (HA)
Nature Adamant (+Atk, -SpA)
EVs 248 HP, 252 Atk, 8 Spe
Moves - Facade
- Poison Jab
- Close Combat
- Swords Dance

Name Ursaring Notes
Item Flame Orb / Sitrus Berry Guts boosts Atk by x1.5 which plays
well with STAB Facade.

Basically a better Zangoose, but still
too squishy to be able to recommend
it for higher difficulty raids.
Tera Normal
Ability Guts
Nature Adamant (+Atk, -SpA)
EVs 204 HP, 252 Atk,
4 Def, 48 Spe
Moves - Facade
- Crunch
- Close Combat
- Belly Drum
